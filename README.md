# 🛒 DMart Sales Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

An end-to-end sales analytics dashboard built in Power BI for DMart retail data, covering customer behavior, product performance, and revenue trends.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

### Page 1 – Main Dashboard
| Visual | Type | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| Total Sales | KPI Card | Overall revenue generated |
| Total Orders | KPI Card | Count of all orders |
| Average Rating | KPI Card | Customer satisfaction score |
| Average Purchase Value | KPI Card | Revenue per transaction |
| Sales by Category | Bar Chart | Revenue breakdown by product category and season |
| Sales by Customer Tag & Gender | Column Chart | Purchase behavior segmented by customer type and gender |
| Sales by Age Category & Payment Method | Column Chart | Revenue by age group and payment mode |
| Top 5 Items | Pivot Table | Highest-selling products by revenue |
| Sales by Product Quality | Pie Chart | Revenue split across product quality tiers |
| Orders by Season | Line Chart | Seasonal trend in total orders |

**Slicers:** Discount | Sales Category | Gender

### Page 2 – Sales Category Breakdown
- Column chart: Sales by Sales Category

### Page 3 – Color Analysis
- Treemap: Product distribution by color

---

## 🧮 Key DAX Measures
- `Total Sales` – Sum of all sales revenue
- `Total Orders` – Count of transactions
- `Average Rating` – Mean customer rating
- `Average Purchase Value` – Revenue ÷ orders

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used
- **Power BI Desktop** – Report design and publishing
- **DAX** – Custom measures and KPI calculations
- **Power Query** – Data transformation and cleaning
- **Data Modeling** – Relationships across sales, customer, and product tables
